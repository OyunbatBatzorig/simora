How it works
01

Company posts a task

Pick a robot, environment, and manipulation goal. Set your budget and episode target.

02

You teleoperate in sim

Open your browser, pick a task, and control the robot. Physics-accurate — gravity, friction, force.

03

We deliver the model

Episodes → behaviour cloning → RL fine-tuning. Company gets a policy. You get paid.
